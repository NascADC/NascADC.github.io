Education

® 2023: He completed his postdoctoral phase at the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the Victoria university of Wellington (VUW), New Zealand. During this time, He coordinated a project involving the UFPE, the VUW, and the Center of Studies in Resources Engineering Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, Mumbai, India.

® 2012: He received his D.Sc. in Statistics from the Federal University of Pernambuco, Recife, Brazil. His dissertation was entitled "Statistical Information Theory for Univariate and Polarimetric Radar Data with Synthetic Aperture". In 2012, his dissertation was recognized as the best doctoral thesis by the Brazilian Society of Applied Informatics Brazilian Society of Applied and Computational Mathematics.

® 2007: He completed his M.Sc. in Statistics at the Federal University of Pernambuco, Recife, Brazil. His thesis was entitled "Contrast measure and stochastic distances in a model for speckled data".

® 2005: He received his B.Sc. in Statistics from the Federal University of Pernambuco, Recife, Brazil. He worked on the following topics, among others Proposal of a new social index from multivariate analysis (final course work), Classification of signatures from wavelet transform (Second scientific introduction) and Pattern recognition with bivariate time series (First scientific introduction).
